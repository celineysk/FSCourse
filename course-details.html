Course Description

Feng Shui Fundamentals is a comprehensive introduction to the ancient Chinese art of harmonizing individuals with their surrounding environment. This course is designed for beginners with no prior knowledge of Feng Shui and provides a solid foundation in both theory and practical application.

Throughout this course, you'll learn how to apply Feng Shui principles to create balance, harmony, and positive energy in any space. You'll discover how the arrangement of objects, colors, and materials can influence various aspects of your life, including health, relationships, career, and prosperity.

Miss Yeoh brings over 20 years of experience as a Feng Shui master and consultant, sharing practical insights that go beyond theory. Each lesson includes real-world examples, case studies, and practical exercises to help you apply what you've learned immediately.

By the end of this course, you'll have the knowledge and confidence to assess and improve the Feng Shui of your home or office, creating spaces that support your goals and well-being.
