About This Lesson

In this lesson, you will learn about the Five Elements theory (Wu Xing) in Feng Shui. The Five Elements—Wood, Fire, Earth, Metal, and Water—are fundamental to understanding how energy flows and interacts in your environment.

Each element has specific characteristics, associations, and relationships with the other elements. Understanding these relationships is essential for creating balance and harmony in your space.
